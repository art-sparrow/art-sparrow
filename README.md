### 💫 About Me

I'm a full-stack mobile engineer with 5+ years shipping production Flutter apps backed by Firebase and RESTful APIs — owning everything from architecture and CI/CD to App Store / Play Store release. I care about clean BLoC architectures, multi-tenant data models, and software that feels considered, not just functional.

I'm also pursuing an **MSc in Computer Science (Machine Learning track)** at **Kenyatta University**, where my research focuses on **ML-driven clinical aftercare** — connecting my mobile/backend background to applied ML in healthcare.

- 🔭 Currently building **Yona** — a Flutter + Firebase companion app for independent service providers (nail artisans, barbers, salons & more)
- 🎓 Currently researching ML for clinical aftercare as part of my MSc thesis, and applying it to an early-stage healthcare companion app
- 🧩 Occasional contributor to the mobile engineering community (`Reddit`, `LinkedIn`, `Blogs`, `Meet & greets`)
- ⚡ Off-screen: nature walks and sandy beaches
